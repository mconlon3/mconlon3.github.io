Website Evaluations

Good Website: Mercari

This is Mercari's website.

  • Contrast: The header is a different color than the rest of the webpage, providing a distinction.
  • Repetition: Mercari sticks to a color pallete of blue and white. The previews for different sections of the website all look similar.
  • Alignment: The header is aligned at the top, text is aligned to the left. The most important categories are near the top.
  • Proximity: Similar categories are grouped together, all the important information like the search bar, login, and cart are in the header.

Bad Website: Spacejam

This is the Spacejam website from 1996.

  • Contrast: This website uses many dark highly saturated colors, the background is very busy.
  • Repetition: Colors are not repeated, every planet looks different. The planet titles are yellow, but every other piece of text is red.
  • Alignment: Everything is centered and the clickable planets are scattered. There is no header or other way of navigating the website.
  • Proximity: The Sitemap is simply another planet and is not distinct in anyway. It should not be grouped with them since it is unique.
