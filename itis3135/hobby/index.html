BUILDING WITH LEGOS

About Me

I have always loved LEGOs as a child. There is a lot of room for creativity because you can build whatever you like, but I also enjoyed building the sets that the company already made.

I am a very creative person, so LEGO came as a natural hobby to me both as a child and as an adult,

What is LEGO?

LEGO is a product for all ages. It is plastic building blocks that can be used to build great creations.

LEGO sells sets of builds you can make, or you can get creative and make your own creation.

Best Times

While there's always time to be creative, it's best to enjoy this activity when you have a lot of freetime.

You can leave it as a daily activity to come home to, making progress day by day.

Best Locations

You will need a lot of open room for most sets. A flat table that won't be used for a while can be a good place to build on.

You should avoid building on the floor as pieces can be lost this way.

How to build with LEGO?

Building LEGOs is very simple as every set comes with a booklet of instructions.

However, the internet is a useful resource to find inspiration for when you want to build something original.

Why build with LEGO?

There is no end goal with LEGO aside from enjoying yourself. This is why it makes a great hobby, as you don't feel like you have to have a reason to sit down and enjoy it.

You could also use the finished product as a goal, giving you a reason to keep building until you're done.
